What is RYUG?

RYUG stands for recycle your ugly gif and we're a type of thrift store. While that may seem a bit harsh one of our founders thought the name rather humourous. She also thought hearing its name would encourage people to donate (aka get rid of) gifts they don't want.

We accept used gifts and will even buy unsed ones from you!
